Words of Eternal Life

"Lord, to whom shall we go? thou hast the words of eternal life. And we believe and are sure that thou art that Christ, the Son of the living God." John 6:68b-69

What is Words of Eternal Life?

Words of Eternal Life is a website designed to give a brief overview of the gospel of Jesus Christ and basic Christian doctrine. The Lord Jesus Christ, before he ascended into heaven after his resurrection from the dead, gave the following commandment to his disciples,

"And Jesus came and spake unto them, saying, All power is given unto me in heaven and in earth. Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost: Teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you: and, lo, I am with you always, even unto the end of the world. Amen." Matthew 28:18-20

This site, therefore, is set up with the purpose of fulfilling his command to teach all nations to observe all things he has commanded us. To learn more about what Christians believe and how you can respond, please click the link below.
