Christian Music

The Bible teaches that our music must glorify God and be filled with the truth of his word that we may be able to teach one another through songs. Therefore, Christian music is not mere entertainment, and it is important that we listen to and sing only songs that have sound doctrine and not mere empty words. It is written,

"Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord." Colossians 3:16

In light of this, here's a small collection of songs that have good theology which we can sing to honour God.
