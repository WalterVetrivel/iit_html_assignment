The Coming of Jesus Christ

The return of Jesus Christ

Jesus is in heaven at the right hand of God

According to the scriptures, Jesus is now seated at the right hand of God in heaven and will be there making intercession for us until he returns to judge the living and the dead.

"Looking unto Jesus the author and finisher of our faith; who for the joy that was set before him endured the cross, despising the shame, and is set down at the right hand of the throne of God." Hebrews 12:2
"Whom the heaven must receive until the times of restitution of all things, which God hath spoken by the mouth of all his holy prophets since the world began." Acts 3:21

The resurrection of the dead

When Christ returns in his glory with all the angels, the dead will be raised and judged. The righteous, who have placed their trust in him for salvation, will inherit eternal life and the rest will face everlasting condemation.

"Marvel not at this: for the hour is coming, in the which all that are in the graves shall hear his voice, And shall come forth; they that have done good, unto the resurrection of life; and they that have done evil, unto the resurrection of damnation." John 5:28-29

The rewarding of the righteous and the punishment of the wicked

The saints will be rewarded at the resurrection whereas the wicked shall be condemned.

"When the Son of man shall come in his glory, and all the holy angels with him, then shall he sit upon the throne of his glory: And before him shall be gathered all nations: and he shall separate them one from another, as a shepherd divideth his sheep from the goats: And he shall set the sheep on his right hand, but the goats on the left. And these shall go away into everlasting punishment: but the righteous into life eternal." Matthew 25:31-33, 46

The tabernacle of God is with man

After the judgment, death and sin will be abolished, and there will be everlasting righteousness and life. God himself will dwell with us and be our God, and we will be his people. A new heavens and a new earth will be created, where dwells righteousness.

"Nevertheless we, according to his promise, look for new heavens and a new earth, wherein dwelleth righteousness." 2 Peter 3:13
"And I heard a great voice out of heaven saying, Behold, the tabernacle of God is with men, and he will dwell with them, and they shall be his people, and God himself shall be with them, and be their God." Revelation 21:3

There will be no more pain or suffering, and the former things will pass away, and God will make all things new. This is our blessed hope, as we will be with our Lord Jesus Christ forever.

"And God shall wipe away all tears from their eyes; and there shall be no more death, neither sorrow, nor crying, neither shall there be any more pain: for the former things are passed away. And he that sat upon the throne said, Behold, I make all things new. And he said unto me, Write: for these words are true and faithful." Revelation 21:4-5
"For the Lord himself shall descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of the archangel, and with the trump of God: and the dead in Christ shall rise first: Then we which are alive and remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds, to meet the Lord in the air: and so shall we ever be with the Lord." 1 Thessalonians 16-17

Summary

To summarise,

  1. Jesus is in heaven at the right hand of God
  2. Christ returns to judge the world
  3. At the judgment, the righteous inherit everlasting life and the wicked enter everlasting punishment
  4. New heavens and a new earth are created, and God dwells with man
